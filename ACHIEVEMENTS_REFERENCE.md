## Design Philosophy

✅ **Progressive Difficulty**
- Clear path from beginner to master
- Each tier 2-3x harder than previous
- No sudden jumps in difficulty

✅ **Multiple Paths to Progression**
- Can focus on: XP, focus time, or quizzes
- Don't need perfection in one category
- Diverse playstyles all rewarded

✅ **Sustainable Engagement**
- Always a "next goal" to pursue
- Takes months/years to see all badges
- Prevents burnout from completion

✅ **Clear Rewards**
- 27 unique emojis and names
- Descriptive names in Uzbek
- Visual progression (colors level 1-50+)
